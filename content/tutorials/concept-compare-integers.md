# Comparing Integers

When you compare integers, numbers farther to the right on the number line are greater.

Tips:
- Positive numbers are greater than zero.
- Zero is greater than all negative numbers.
- Among negative numbers, the one closer to zero is greater.

Example:
- `-3` is greater than `-8`
- `4` is greater than `-1`
